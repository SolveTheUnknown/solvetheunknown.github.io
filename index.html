Why did I create SolveTheUnknown?

I've always loved tech, ever since I was a little kid. As I grew up I encoutered more problems, and more solutions. Most of the time I would have to spend hours looking for a solution that did not even work!

So, I created SolveTheUnknown, where you can come to find solutions to problems that are highly uncommon, and if its not here, you can ASK! Just visit the "Ask A Question" page, and we will guide you from there!
